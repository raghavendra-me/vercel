Top Courses in France 2025

Published on February 11, 2025

France continues to be a global leader in higher education, attracting students from around the world with its top-tier institutions. Some academic disciplines remain evergreen, meaning they consistently offer long-term career prospects, adaptability, and global demand. Whether you aspire to become a business leader, an engineer, a fashion expert, a healthcare professional, or a hospitality specialist, France provides an excellent learning environment with practical exposure. Here's a look at the best courses to study in France in 2025 and why they are a smart career investment.

Business and Management

Business and Management remain at the heart of global economies, making them one of the most reliable career paths. In France, students gain valuable insights into international business, marketing, finance, and data analytics, learning from industry experts and engaging in case studies that reflect real-world market trends. As France is the hub of multinational companies, financial centres, and start-ups, graduates are strongly placed to work in various fields like consulting, banking, supply chain management, and entrepreneurship. The combination of theory and real-world projects makes business graduates competent enough to succeed in a competitive global economy.Below are the few top universities for Business:

  • HEC Paris (Among the top in Europe)
  • INSEAD (Renowned globally for MBA Programs)
  • ESSEC Business School
  • EDHEC Business School
  • Grenoble Ecole de Management

Popular courses are Grande école, Bachelors/Masters in International Business, M.Sc. in Innovation and Entrepreneurship, MBA in International Finance, M.Sc. in Big data & Business Analytics.

Engineering

Engineering and Technology are driving forces of current growth, and they are a necessity in today's rapidly changing industries. With a particular focus on research, sustainability, and new technologies, students gain practical experience in state-of-the-art laboratories and partnerships with leading engineering companies. With the growing emphasis on automation, climate change, and digital transformation, engineering graduates have vast career prospects in private organisations as well as government sectors. Students gain hands – on experience through internships and projects in real world industries. The top leading engineering schools:

  • École Polytechnique
  • CentraleSupélec
  • Institut National des Sciences Appliquées (INSA Lyon)
  • École des Ponts Paris Tech
  • Mines Paris Tech

France has been at the forefront of innovation, with specialized courses in aerospace, artificial intelligence, robotics, renewable energy and mechanical engineering.

Arts and Fashion

Arts and Fashion have long made up the face of France's culture and are some of the most appealing courses among the creative-minded. France, especially Paris, is regarded as the fashion capital of the world and a home for several of the most influential luxury brands.. It will be provided with a promising academic and industry experience within a global fashion, working with important brands, artistic skills with academic and professional mentoring while studying in France. With the continuous expansion of digital media, branding, and e-commerce, creative professionals with a background in design and fashion are in high demand globally.Top arts and fashion schools:

  • École des Beaux-Arts (Paris) – Premier Fine Arts School
  • Institut Français de la Mode (IFM) – Leading in Fashion & Design Management
  • ESMOD International
  • La Chambre Syndicate de la Couture Parisienne

Famous courses such as Fashion Design, Luxury Brand Management and Fine Arts are incredibly popular among the international students which offers access to top industry leaders, creative workshops and exposure to global trends.

Healthcare and Sciences

The healthcare industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors in the world, with a high demand for medical research, biotechnology, and public health experts. With increasing investments in biotechnology, medical technology, and global healthcare solutions, professionals in this field contribute to improving public health and developing medical research. Students acquire practical experience through internships, hospital collaborations, and research projects, ensuring that they are prepared for healthcare, medical research, and pharmaceuticals industries. France's affordable education system makes high-quality medical training accessible to international students. Top Healthcare and Sciences Universities:

  • Université Paris Descartes – Focus on Medicine & Life Sciences
  • Université de Strasbourg – Known for Biotechnology and Medicine
  • Université de Montpellier – Expertise in Health Sciences
  • Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1

France is known for its medical research, pharmaceutical innovations, and public health policies, offering top-tier education in biomedical engineering, pharmaceutical sciences, and healthcare management.

Hospitality and Culinary Arts

Hospitality and culinary arts have always been the strong pillars of the economy in France, making them excellent study options for those passionate about luxury service and gastronomy. The international luxury hotel and fine dining industries are booming, and skilled managers are needed to run such businesses. France boasts some of the most prestigious hotels, Michelin-starred restaurants, and well-known culinary institutions in the world. The courses in hotel management, gastronomy, and pastry arts are oriented toward practical training, customer service excellence and business understanding. The hospitality industry is evolving, focusing on sustainable tourism, smart technology, and unique guest experiences. This growth creates exciting opportunities for graduates in luxury hotels, travel businesses and entrepreneurship.Top schools for Hospitality and Culinary arts:

  • Institut Paul Bocuse – Leading Hospitality and Culinary School
  • Le Cordon Bleu Paris – World-renowned culinary institute
  • École Hôtelière de Lausanne (partnered with French institutions)

Top courses in Hospitality and Culinary arts are culinary leadership and innovation, Hotel management, Sustainable tourism and Luxury brand hospitality.

France offers a wide range of diverse and specialized programs beyond these top courses, catering to various career aspirations and industries. Whether you seek niche expertise or interdisciplinary learning, there are countless opportunities to explore.
