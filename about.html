Who We Are

Think back to when you first decided to study abroad—the excitement, the possibilities, the thrill of a new beginning. But then reality hits: moving to a new country isn't just about classes and sightseeing. It's also about navigating a maze of practical steps: visa processing, opening a bank account, finding the right place to live, and getting set up with all the basics. Suddenly, those checklists get long, and everything feels a bit overwhelming.

That's where I2F comes in. We're a group of students who once stood exactly where you are now, feeling both the excitement and the worries. And we created I2F to be the friend we wish we had back then—the one who'd help make everything clearer, simpler, and a lot less intimidating. I2F pairs you with a student partner who's not just a guide but a peer who's been through it all. From choosing a SIM card to opening a bank account, we're here to help you get it done, step by step.

Our Story

Our journey to France started just like yours, filled with hope, but also a fair share of confusion and questions. We remember our own frantic Google searches, those "what do I do now?" moments, and the long lists of must-do tasks, which only seemed to grow as departure day got closer. And when we finally arrived? It was an adventure—but with more bumps than we expected. Figuring out bureaucracy, especially in French, was like solving a never-ending puzzle.

After stumbling through it ourselves, we realized that there had to be a better way. We wanted support that wasn't overly formal or expensive—someone who understood the small specific things only a student would know. More than anything, we wished for someone who'd been in our shoes, who could guide us like a friend rather than a service.

And that's why we started I2F. With us, you don't have to navigate this transition alone. From the moment you decide to study in France to the day you're comfortably settled in, we're here to make sure that everything is handled—without the headaches. We're committed to making your journey to France smoother and more enjoyable, covering each of those "must-do" steps with real, personalized support.

"With I2F, you're not just getting a service; you're getting a friend who understands what it's like to start fresh in a new country."

We're here to make sure you're not just prepared but truly ready for this exciting adventure ahead!
