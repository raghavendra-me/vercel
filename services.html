Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visa requirements for Indian students?

Indian students need a long-stay visa for studies, acceptance letter, proof of finances, and health insurance.

How long does it take to learn French?

Most students reach B2 level in 6-12 months with intensive study.

Are there job opportunities for Indians in France?

Yes, especially in IT, engineering, and research. French language skills are often required.

What's the cost of living for Indian students?

Budget 600-800 euros monthly in smaller cities, 800-1000 euros in Paris.
